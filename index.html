About Us

St. Joseph Catholic Church PhnomPenh, Cambodia

The presence of the Catholic Church in Cambodia has a complex history marked by periods of growth, decline, and resurgence.

Over the years, St. Joseph Church has witnessed significant historical events and undergone several transformations. During the Khmer Rouge regime (1975-1979), like other religious institutions in Cambodia, St. Joseph Church faced persecution, and its activities were severely restricted. Many priests and religious workers were targeted and killed, and the church itself suffered damage.
